OUR STORY

PIT STOP FOR FOOD LOVERS

Formula One Mediterranean Pitstop combines the adrenaline of motorsports with authentic Tunisian hospitality. Located in the heart of Hergla Park, our restaurant celebrates the precision of F1 engineering with the rich flavors of Mediterranean cuisine.

Our chefs work with the precision of a pit crew, delivering dishes inspired by Tunisian culinary traditions. Every plate is crafted with the same attention to detail that goes into building a championship-winning race car, using fresh local ingredients from Sousse's vibrant markets.
